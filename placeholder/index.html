FAQ

Here are the answers to some common questions about InviroConnect! If you don't find the answer you're looking for, please contact us for more information.

Who can use InviroConnect?

InviroConnect works with lots of different people wanting to come together for the Earth. We especially encourage you to set up an account if you are a university student, a nonprofit, a government agency, or a business interested in supporting environmental causes.

How do I sign up?

InviroConnect is still in the development phase right now, but we expect it to be launched by January of 2016. E-mail us here if you'd like us to contact you when the full site is online. Be sure to note if you'd like to be considered as a beta tester!

What is your pricing model?

In order to have our customers pay the smallest amount possible, we aren't announcing pricing until the site is more fully implemented. We are committed to keeping the cost of participation very affordable. Please contact us if you'd like us to let you know when more information is available.

What locations does InviroConnect serve?

Our pilot region is Oregon. However, we intend to expand into other areas as well. If you aren't in Oregon, please contact us and ask us to keep you in the loop!

Can this be used to organize for other causes?

You will be able to do that in the future, but right now, InviroConnect is focusing on environmental issues, because they're what we know best.
